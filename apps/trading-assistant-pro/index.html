Product story

Designed to make the workflow feel effortless.

Trading Assistant Pro is a smart trading workspace for market analysis, watchlists, risk planning, and trade journaling.

Built with a professional dark interface, Trading Assistant Pro brings together live market charts, watchlist management, technical analysis, risk calculation, and trade notes in one clean desktop dashboard.

Whether you are reviewing crypto pairs, checking momentum, or preparing a possible setup, the app helps you stay focused and organized without unnecessary complexity.

It includes candlestick charts, live refresh, customizable watchlists, analysis panels, setup levels, risk/reward calculation, and a trade journal for managing your trading ideas clearly and efficiently.
