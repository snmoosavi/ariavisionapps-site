Product story

Stop losing useful things you already copied.

Clipboard Universal turns the Windows clipboard into an organized history for text, images, audio, video, PDFs, and files. It is useful for users who copy information all day and need to find it again quickly.

Running quietly in the system tray, it keeps clipboard items available without interrupting the current task. That makes it practical for support work, research, writing, development, and office workflows.

The goal is simple: keep frequently reused content close, searchable, and ready to paste when needed.
