Product story

Designed to make the workflow feel effortless.

Trading Journal helps traders plan, execute, and improve with discipline.

Built for Windows, Trading Journal provides a clean workspace for trade planning, risk control, journaling, and performance review. Use a structured pre-trade checklist, calculate position size based on equity, risk percentage, entry, and stop, and manage your trading sessions with practical guardrails such as daily loss limit, max trades per day, and cooldown after consecutive losses.

The app includes a Trade Setup Quality Score that evaluates each setup from 0 to 100 and shows a clear Weak, Medium, or Strong result. You can copy the setup summary or save it directly to your journal.

Record trades with entry, stop, target, quantity, PnL, R-multiple, setup tags, emotions, notes, and before/after screenshots. Review your progress with reports, win rate, profit factor, expectancy, drawdown, and equity curve.
