Product story

Designed to make the workflow feel effortless.

Voice to Text Converter Pro converts audio and video files into accurate, editable text with support for online recognition and offline Whisper transcription.

Use it for meetings, lectures, podcasts, interviews, tutorials, and video content. After transcription, you can edit the text, translate it, create subtitles, and export your work as TXT, DOCX, PDF, SRT, or WebVTT.

The new Export Pack saves everything in one organized package: transcript files, subtitle files, PDF/DOCX exports, translation files when available, and a manifest. With improved video handling, recent files, dark/light themes, and a refined Windows interface, the app gives you a focused workflow from media file to usable text.
