Product story

Move spoken content into usable text.

Voice to Text Converter Pro helps convert meetings, lectures, interviews, podcasts, tutorials, and videos into text that can be edited, translated, subtitled, and exported.

It supports a workflow from media file to transcript, subtitle files, document exports, and organized output packs. That makes it useful for creators, students, researchers, and teams that work with recorded speech.

Instead of juggling separate tools for transcription, subtitles, and exports, users can manage the process in one desktop app.

Why users choose it

Built for media-to-text workflows.

  • Convert audio and video into editable transcripts
  • Create subtitle files for video workflows
  • Export text into practical document formats
  • Organize transcript, subtitle, and translation files together
  • Useful for meetings, courses, interviews, and content production
