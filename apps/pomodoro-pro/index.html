Product story

Turn focus time into a clear daily rhythm.

Pomodoro Pro helps professionals, students, and remote workers turn the day into manageable focus blocks. Build reusable time-blocking plans, run task sequences, and keep progress visible while you work.

Custom durations, goals, streaks, notification sounds, history charts, and the floating Mini HUD make it easier to stay aware of time without constantly opening the main window.

It is built for users who want structure without clutter: plan the day, start the timer, and keep attention on the work that matters.
