Product story

Designed to make the workflow feel effortless.

Noise Remover helps you clean audio files offline with a simple, focused workflow.

Remove background noise from voice recordings, interviews, podcasts, lectures, music samples, and older audio files without requiring an internet connection.

Select a noise-only part of the waveform, preview the original and cleaned audio with the built-in A/B player, then export the improved result in WAV, MP3, or FLAC.

The app includes Speech and Music modes, smart cleanup presets, Noise Gate, 50/60 Hz Hum Removal, De-click/De-pop, output peak normalization, batch export, and a cleanup quality report for better control over your final audio.

Designed for privacy, simplicity, and reliable offline use.
